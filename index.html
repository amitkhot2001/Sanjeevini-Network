WHAT WE ARE DOING

Sanjeevini HIV AIDS Sonkitha Vekthigala Sangha's Commitment to Individuals Living with HIV

Sanjeevini HIV AIDS Sonkitha Vekthigala Sangha, Davangere Founded in 2006, this organization has spent 18 years enhancing the lives of individuals living with HIV in the district, providing vital social, economic, and educational support to foster a healthier, empowered community.

The organization addresses social stigma surrounding individuals living with HIV by promoting inclusivity, enabling them to thrive in employment, education, and healthcare. By challenging discrimination and fostering self-worth, it empowers those affected to lead fulfilling and dignified lives.

The organization steadfastly advocates for the employment rights of individuals living with HIV, intervening to support those facing discrimination or unfair dismissal. It promotes inclusivity in the workplace by providing training and counseling to employers and colleagues, emphasizing the need to understand HIV and treat affected individuals with dignity.

The organization is dedicated to ensuring equal access to education for children living with HIV. It collaborates with schools and colleges to eliminate stigma and discrimination, conducting awareness campaigns and advocating for inclusive policies. This effort aims to create a supportive learning environment where these children can thrive academically and reach their full potential.

Supporting HIV-Affected Individuals During the Pandemic

During the COVID pandemic, our organization supported vulnerable communities by distributing ration kits, thanks to the generous support of Mr. Ajim Premji. We also provided ART (Antiretroviral Therapy) tablets to individuals living with HIV in Davangere.

Our team engaged directly with the community, ensuring those in need received essential medication and support. These efforts aimed to address immediate needs while fostering hope and resilience, reflecting our commitment to building a healthier and more inclusive society during challenging times.

Our Programs

Supporting HIV Patients

Sanjeevini HIV AIDS Sonkitha Vekthigala Sangha is dedicated to supporting those in need by providing ART tablets directly to HIV patients' homes. This essential service is offered across all talukas of Davanagere, ensuring that individuals receive the medication they require for their health and well-being. Through this initiative, we aim to enhance access to treatment and improve the quality of life for our community members.

Supporting Child Nutrition and Health

At Sanjeevini HIV AIDS Sonkitha Vekthigala Sangha, our Child Nutrition and Health Care Program is dedicated to enhancing the health of children. We provide essential nutrition education and resources to families, ensuring children receive the vital nutrients for healthy growth. Through workshops and community outreach, we raise awareness about proper dietary practices and health care. Our team collaborates with local health services to improve access to medical care and nutritional support for vulnerable families, empowering the next generation for a healthier future.

Summer Camp

The summer camp program by Sanjeevini HIV AIDS Sonkitha Vekthigala Sangha promotes equality among HIV-positive and HIV-negative children. By creating a safe and inclusive environment, we foster friendships and understanding between the children. Through engaging activities and workshops, we break down stigma and encourage mutual respect. Our goal is to empower all children, emphasizing that every child deserves love and care, regardless of their health status.
