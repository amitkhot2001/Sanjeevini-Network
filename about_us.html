The CBO, Our Hand For Help Charitable Sanjeevini HIV/AIDS Sonkitha Vekthigala Sangha, is consistently working for those among us who are not blessed with the amenities we often take for granted. Our beneficiaries include the poor, orphaned children, elderly individuals abandoned by their families, and those who are physically and mentally challenged.
